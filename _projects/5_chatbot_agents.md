Worked at **DataBoss Inc.** under Prof. S. Serdar Kozat on AI-powered chatbots and agent assistants for industrial applications, using Langchain.

## Highlights

- Designed and shipped AI-driven chatbots and agent assistants for varied production purposes.
- Developed multiple **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** variants and agent architectures, including a **self-multi-RAG** built on top of self-RAG to improve information retrieval and response quality.
- Explored agent-orchestration patterns balancing latency, factuality, and cost.

This experience grounded my research in the practical realities of LLM deployment — what breaks at scale, which ablations matter, and where evaluation is hardest.
